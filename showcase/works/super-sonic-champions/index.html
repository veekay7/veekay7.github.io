Overview

Our partnership with Bloodhound SSC has given us a fascinating insight into what it takes to reach 1000mph. Other the last few months we have worked closely with the team to reimagine land speed records for the current generation.

During my internship at PlayWest UK, my primary responsibility was to convert the physics and algorithms of the super sonic car, originally written in JavaScript, to C# in Unity while making use of Unity's physics system. Later on, I set up the game to work with the Oculus Rift VR and also tweaked the controls to work better with VR.
